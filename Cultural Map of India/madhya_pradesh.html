Traditional Foods

Poha

A light breakfast dish made of flattened rice, spices, and onions, often served with jalebi.

Bhutte Ka Kees

A popular street food made of grated corn cooked with milk and spices – a true Indore specialty.

Dal Bafla

A traditional wheat dish similar to Rajasthan’s dal baati, served with ghee and spicy dal.

Traditional Dresses

Chanderi Saree

Elegant handwoven sarees from Chanderi town, known for lightweight texture and zari work.

Dhoti Kurta

The traditional men’s attire in rural Madhya Pradesh, reflecting simplicity and comfort.

Festivals of Madhya Pradesh

Lokrang Festival

A celebration of folk dances, music, and crafts organized every year in Bhopal.

Khajuraho Dance Festival

A world-famous classical dance event held against the backdrop of Khajuraho temples.

Bhagoria Festival

Bhagoria is a vibrant tribal festival of Madhya Pradesh, celebrated with music, dance, and colors, symbolizing love and community joy before Holi.

Traditional Art

Gond Art

A tribal art form that uses intricate lines and dots to depict nature and mythology.

Bhil Art

Vibrant paintings by the Bhil tribe, known for symbolic dots and folk storytelling themes.
