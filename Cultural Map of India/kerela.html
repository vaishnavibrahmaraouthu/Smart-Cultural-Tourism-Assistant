Festivals of kerala

Onam

The grand harvest festival celebrated with flower carpets, feasts, and traditional games.

Vishu

Kerala’s New Year festival symbolizing prosperity and new beginnings.

Thrissur pooram

A spectacular temple festival known for decorated elephants, percussion music, and fireworks.

Traditional Art

Kathakali

A classical dance-drama combining elaborate makeup, costumes, and expressive gestures to tell epic tales.

Theyyam

A ritual dance form showcasing divine legends with vibrant costumes and powerful performances.

Mural paintings

Intricate temple wall art depicting mythological stories in vivid natural colors.
